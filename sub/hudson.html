Hudson Table

A culinary studio. An event space. A commercial kitchen. Melding these programs are fun for the architects, but a challenge for the mechanical engineers. By utilizing a series of VRV air handlers and some ingenuity, this space stays as fresh during Texas Steak Night as it does during birthday parties.

A design/build project.

Teammates included Urban Design Workshop,Design Compendium, and Brian Trematore.
