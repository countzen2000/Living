Edge Lofts

The first LEED Platinum project in Hoboken, the architecture and engineering of the Edge Lofts compliments the needs and wants of the brightest urban dweller. Photovoltaic arrays, sunshades, and enhanced indoor ventilation systems round out the sustainable kit of parts that pushed this project past its competitors.

A design/build project.

Teammates included MHS Architects, Jochen Spengler, and Steven Winters Associates.
